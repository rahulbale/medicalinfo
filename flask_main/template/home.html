Less of Doctors: India is facing a huge shortage of Doctors.

The shortage of doctors, nurses, technicians and infrastructure as given by the report of WHO that 0.75% doctors and 2.09% nurses are present per 1000 people. Additionally, Indian healthcare is also facing acute shortage of hospital beds with 1.3% hospital beds per 1000 population.

Low Accessibility: Less Accessibility to Healthcare across nation.

The physical access to hospitals is still the major problem to both Rural and the Urban India. By making use of web technology and AI people in remote areas can also get access to the health-care in free.

It is the biggest problem many Poor and people with unavailablity of money are hit the most as by the government estimates, approx 63 million people are faced with poverty every year because of their healthcare expenditure.
